Validating Web Forms

First name be alphanumeric and contaion 3 - 16 characters

Last name be alphanumeric and contaion 3 - 16 characters

Email must be a valid address, e.g. example@example.com

Password must be alphanumeric (@,_and - are also allowed) and between 6 - 20 characters

A valid Telephone number (11 digits and 333-333-3334)

Bio must contaion only lowercase letters, underscore, hyphens and 8 - 50 characters
